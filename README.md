## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as **Task 2** of the **Data Science & Analytics Internship** at **Future Interns**.

The objective was to analyze customer churn behavior using the **Telco Customer Churn Dataset** and develop an interactive Excel dashboard that enables stakeholders to identify churn patterns, understand customer retention drivers, evaluate revenue impact, and support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The analysis aims to answer the following business questions:

- Why are customers leaving?
- Which customer segments have the highest churn risk?
- Which factors influence customer retention?
- What is the financial impact of customer churn?
- What business actions can reduce customer loss?

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit the highest churn rate.
- **Short-tenure customers** are significantly more likely to churn.
- Customers using **Fiber Optic Internet** experience higher churn than DSL users.
- **Electronic Check** is the payment method most frequently associated with customer churn.
- Customers on **One-Year and Two-Year contracts** demonstrate substantially higher retention.
- Senior Citizens show comparatively higher churn than other customer groups.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Promote long-term contracts through targeted retention offers.
- Improve onboarding during the first few months of customer subscription.
- Develop proactive retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
- Enhance customer support quality for Fiber Optic users.
- Introduce loyalty rewards for long-tenure customers.
- Continuously monitor churn trends using interactive dashboards.
